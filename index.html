Introduction

Why should you not use Optifine? In short: - It gives a significantly smaller performance boost compared to mods like Sodium (less FPS gain) - Worse compatibility with other mods - Better and more configurable graphic features Despite having been a popular performance mod for many years you might be surprised to learn that in recent updates Optifine does little in terms of performance. Moreover its many graphical related have caused many issues often leading to a significant degrade in performance. Here we'll showcase the state of the art performance and graphical enhancement scenario in a way that everyone could understand.
